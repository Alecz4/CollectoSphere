Shipping & Delivery

At Collecto Sphere, we are committed to delivering your favorite collectibles in a timely and secure manner. Below are the details of our shipping and delivery process.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

2. Shipping Methods & Time

We offer various shipping options, including standard and express delivery. Estimated delivery times:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping costs vary based on the destination and selected shipping method. The shipping fee will be calculated at checkout.

4. International Shipping

We offer international shipping to selected countries. Delivery times vary based on location, and additional customs fees may apply.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor the status of your shipment.

6. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive within the estimated time, please contact our support team at contact@collectosphere.com.

7. Contact Us

For any shipping-related inquiries, feel free to reach out to us at contact@collectosphere.com.
